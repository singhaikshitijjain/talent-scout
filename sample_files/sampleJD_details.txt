Job Title
AI/ML Engineer

Job Description
We are looking for a skilled AI/ML Engineer to design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for real-world applications. You will work closely with data scientists, backend engineers, and product teams to build scalable AI systems. The role involves working on NLP, computer vision, and predictive analytics use cases, optimizing models for performance, and integrating them into production environments.

Requirements
Strong proficiency in Python and libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn
Experience with deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch
Knowledge of NLP (transformers, embeddings, LLMs) or Computer Vision
Experience with REST APIs, FastAPI or Flask
Familiarity with databases (SQL/NoSQL)
Understanding of data structures and algorithms
Experience in model deployment (Docker, APIs, cloud platforms like AWS/GCP/Azure)
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, AI, or related field
Nice to Have
Experience with LLMs (OpenAI, Hugging Face, Ollama)
Knowledge of MLOps tools (MLflow, Airflow, Kubeflow)
Experience with vector databases (FAISS, Pinecone, Weaviate)
Familiarity with frontend frameworks like React
Contributions to open-source AI projects
Experience with real-time systems or streaming data (Kafka, Spark)
